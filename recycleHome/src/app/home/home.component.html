Lafayette Recycling Initiative


Keeping Lafayette Clean



According to a study by Eunomia, Louisiana ranked 49th out of the 50 states in recycling efforts at only a 4% recycling rate. The Lafayette Recycling Initiative is a citywide project geared towards raising that rate and increasing recycling throughout Lafayette.



The Lafayette Recycling Initiative offers information on the city of Lafayette’s municipal recycling collection. We also provide information on recycling drop-off locations. These drop-off locations are used by all Louisiana residents and visitors.



We also have the Lafayette Recycling Initiative rewards program! This program is a collaboration with local sponsors that rewards residents for engaging in the Lafayette Recycling Initiative collection program. Earn points for recycling and creating a cleaner Lafayette tomorrow.



Lafayette Recycling

In 2021, the recycling rate for Lafayette Parish was 21.42%, down 2% from the previous year. The Lafayette Recycling Initiative is a citywide project geared towards raising that rate and increasing recycling throughout Lafayette.

Municipal Recycling

The City of Lafayette Curbside Recycling Program is available to all Lafayette residents who have the "total environmental charge" on their monthly Lafayette Utilities System bill. Registered residents will receive one recycling bin free of charge.



Registration for recycling can be completed by calling 337-205-7710 or going to this page.



The Curbside Recycling Program only accepts clean flattened cardboard, paper, metal cans, and rigid plastic containers.





Republic Services will conduct bulky waste pickup services one week per month. Visit the City of Lafayette’s Curbside Collection Schedule page to find your collection week.

Household Hazardous Waste Day

Household Hazardous Waste Day is a biannual event hosted by Lafayette Parish Public Works Department in partnership with CGI. During the June 2022 Household Hazardous Waste Day, 20 tons of hazardous waste were collected. A date and location for upcoming Hazardous Waste Events will be posted near the event date.
